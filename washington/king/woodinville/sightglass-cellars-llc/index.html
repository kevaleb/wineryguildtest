Sightglass Cellars is a new Washington winery owned by Sean and his wife, Kristin Jostad.

They are introducing Sightglass Cellars as one of Washington’s newest and most promising wineries. Their goal is to maximize the expression of Washington’s best grapes in a series of distinctive wines to include Chardonnay, Cabernet Franc, Cabernet Sauvignon, a Grenache and Syrah blend, plus a future Sauvignon Blanc.
